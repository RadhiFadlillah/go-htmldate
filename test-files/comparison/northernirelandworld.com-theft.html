Woman fined for theft of 'cat treats'

A woman who admitted stealing 'cat treats' worth £11.55 has been fined £70.
By Antrim Court Reporter
Published 9th Nov 2023, 09:34 GMT

Caitlin McCrory (22), of New Street in Randalstown, committed theft from a shop in the town on August 11 this year.

The case was mentioned at Antrim Magistrates Court, sitting in Ballymena, on Tuesday, November 7.
